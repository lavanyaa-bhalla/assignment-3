FAQs

Do I need to pay to register?
Most events are free. Some workshops may have a small fee — check announcements.
How do I form a team?
In the registration form, list team members separated by commas. One member registers the team.
How do I get certificates?
Participants and winners will receive digital certificates via email within 2 weeks after the event.
