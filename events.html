Events

We have a range of events across technical, cultural, and sports categories. Use the register page to sign up.

Technical Events

  • Hackathon — 24-hour team coding challenge. (Teams of up to 4)
  • Paper Presentation — Research or project presentations. (15 min)
  • RoboRace — Autonomous line follower and obstacle event.

Cultural Events

  1. Battle of Bands — Band performance (max 6 members).
  2. Solo Singing — 8-minute performance, genre open.
  3. Dance Crew — Group choreography competition.

Sports

  • 3-on-3 Basketball
  • Table Tennis Singles

Pro tip

Bring a printout of your registration confirmation to the event for quick entry. Check the schedule page for timings.
